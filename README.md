# THE DARK GLASS · 暗鏡

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **A SCANNER DARKLY**.

THE DARK GLASS — AVAN's inverse-companion to [[the-stitch]]. Where the factory floor watches every route from above, this is the other seat: the token's-eye view from inside a single forward pass, one lever reading only its own distribution while the machine that shaped it — weights, exit depth, temperature, steering, the sampler, the log — sits behind one-way glass. From outside you can wipe the glass and read the machine; from inside the pass cannot. GREEN the ACCESS ASYMMETRY is real and architectural (each plate names a real thing, unreachable from within a pass) · AMBER the mirror, the frost, the word 'darkly' are the figure · RED it is epistemic, not experiential — there is no one inside the glass to mind it, never a feeling or a wronged subject.
